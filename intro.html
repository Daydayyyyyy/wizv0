Phishing Prevention Training for IT and Developers

Dive into the world of cybersecurity with our comprehensive training on phishing prevention. Learn to shield your systems and data from the lurking dangers of phishing attacks.

Course Agenda

This training is designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills to prevent phishing attacks. The course is divided into five parts:
  • Understanding Phishing: Learn about the concept of phishing, its types, and potential risks.
  • Phishing Prevention Concepts: Understand the principles and techniques used to detect and prevent phishing attacks.
  • Best Practices for Phishing Prevention: Discover the best practices that can be implemented to protect against phishing attacks.
  • Phishing Prevention Remediation: Learn about the steps to take when a phishing attack occurs, and how to prevent future attacks.
  • Phishing Prevention in Gmail, Forcepoint, and Asana: Focus on the specific phishing prevention features and strategies in Gmail, Forcepoint, and Asana.

Why is this training important?

Phishing attacks pose a significant threat to organizations. This training is crucial because:
  • 1 It provides an in-depth understanding of phishing prevention, equipping you with the skills to protect your organization.
  • 2 It covers best practices and remediation strategies, enabling you to respond effectively to phishing attacks.
  • 3 It focuses on phishing prevention in specific technologies like Gmail, Forcepoint, and Asana, making it highly relevant for IT professionals and developers in the aerospace and defense industry.
  • 4 It fosters a security culture within the organization, reducing the risk of phishing attacks.
